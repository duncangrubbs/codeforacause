COVID-19 Daily Digest

Welcome, to your COVID-19 daily digest; a central location for up-to-date COVID-19 information, adjusted to your needs. Mental health is incredibly important in times like these. Eye-grabbing headlines and global statistics can be damaging when seen every day. Here, you can choose how much you want to see, so you can stay informed while remaining sane. Below, you can choose your filter setting. Basic provides only local numbers. General includes US numbers along with local data. Extensive adds news and science articles to the comprehensive data.
